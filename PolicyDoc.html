    1. Confidentiality and Covenant Not to Compete:


    For good consideration and as an inducement for Company to employ Employee, if such employment is terminated for any cause, employee shall not, for a period of one (1) year after termination of his/her employment, engage directly or indirectly, either personally or as an employee, associate partner, partner, or otherwise, or by means of any corporate or other device.


    2. Trade Secrets & Confidential Data:

    The Employee acknowledges that the Company shall or may in reliance of this agreement provide Employee access to trade secrets, customers, leads and other confidential data and good will. Employee agrees to retain said information as confidential and not to use said information on his or her own behalf or disclose same to any third party directly or indirectly. The Employee will take necessary actions to keep the Company's business secrets, including but not limited to customer lists, supplier details, logistical, financial, research and development information, confidential and not to disclose the Company's business secrets to any third party during and after the term of the Employee's employment


    3. Specific Account Non-Competition Clause:

    On the termination of the Employee's employment with the Company for any reason, the Employee shall not solicit any customer of the Company that was a customer or prospect of the Company during the course of the Employee's employment with the Company, whether or not still a customer of the Company and whether or not knowledge of the customer is considered confidential information, or in any way aid and assist any other person to solicit any such customer for a period of one year from the date of termination of the Employee's employment.


    5. Indemnification:

    Employee4agrees to pay liquidated damages to the company for breach or contravention of any of the covenants or conditions in this agreement.


    6. Binding Agreement:

    If any part of these promises is void for any reason, the undersigned accepts that it may be severed without affecting the validity or enforceability of the balance of the promises. Notwithstanding the termination of the employment of the Employee for any reason whatsoever, this agreement shall be in force and shall be binding on the Employee for a period of two years from the date of such termination of his employment. This agreement shall be binding upon and inure to the benefit of the parties, their successors, assigns, and personal representatives.
